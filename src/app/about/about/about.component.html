About us

Surat Tamil Sangam was established in 1970 and has become a recognized organization of Tamil community by virtue of its principled approach in upholding the customs and culture of Tamilnadu.
Surat Tamil Sangam strives to bring all Tamilians and Tamil speaking well wishers under one umbrella and propagates and spreads the culture and customs by organizing various functions and celebrating important festivals of Tamilnadu.
The Sangam unites Tamil speaking people and make their presence identified in Surat city. Over the past 37 years the Sangam was led and managed by well learned and experienced members.
